PHASE | Achilles Rehab Foundation | minimum 6 weeks | sequence every 1 day | morning | push | manual
REVIEW | Has morning soreness remained stable or improved for two weeks and have all exercises remained at 3 out of 10 pain or less?
DAY 1 | Heavy Strength
EXERCISE | Straight-Knee Single-Leg Calf Raise | 4 sets x 6-8 reps per leg | required | Use heavy weight and lower for 3 seconds
EXERCISE | Bent-Knee Single-Leg Calf Raise | 4 sets x 6-8 reps per leg | required | Use heavy weight and lower for 3 seconds
EXERCISE | Single-Leg Balance | 3 sets x 45 seconds per leg | required | Maintain steady balance without touching down
EXERCISE | Toe Yoga | 2 minutes | required | Move the big toe independently from the other toes
EXERCISE | Foot Doming | 2 sets x 15 reps | required | Keep toes relaxed while raising the arch
